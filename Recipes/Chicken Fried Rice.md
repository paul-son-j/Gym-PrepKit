# Healthy Chicken & Vegetable Fried Rice (4 Meals)

A balanced **high-protein fried rice meal prep** with chicken, eggs, and vegetables.

---

## Ingredients

### Protein
- 500 g chicken breast (cut into small cubes)
- 3 eggs

### Rice
- 500 g cooked and water soaked old rice

### Vegetables
- 200 g carrots (diced)
- 200 g green beans (diced)
- 200 g cabbage (shredded)
- 1 large capsicum / bell pepper (sliced)
- 1 large onion (minced)
- spring onions (optional, minced)

### Aromatics
- 4 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1 inch ginger (minced)
- Alternative, ginger garlic paste (1 tbsp)

### Cooking Oil/Butter
- 2 tbsp olive oil or sesame oil 

---

## Preparation

### 1. Cook the Rice
1. Wash the rice thoroughly.
2. Cook **500 g rice** in about **900 ml water**.
3. Once cooked, spread it on a plate and let it cool (this helps prevent sticky fried rice).
4. Soak and refrigerate it for a few hours or overnight for better texture.

---

### 2. Cook the Chicken
1. Heat **1 tbsp oil/butter** in a large pan or wok.
2. Add diced chicken.
3. Season with a little salt and pepper.
4. Cook for **5–7 minutes** until fully cooked.
5. Remove and keep aside.

---

### 3. Scramble the Eggs
1. Crack **3 eggs** into the pan.
2. Scramble lightly.
3. Remove and keep aside.

---

### 4. Cook the Vegetables
1. Add **1 tbsp oil/butter** to the pan.
2. Add minced onion, sauté until translucent.
3. Add **garlic, ginger**, sauté for **30 seconds**.
4. Add vegetables in this order:
   - carrots
   - beans
   - cabbage
   - capsicum
5. Stir fry for **3–4 minutes** (keep them slightly crunchy).

---

### 5. Combine Everything
1. Add the cooked rice.
2. Add the chicken and scrambled eggs.
3. Pour in **soy sauce**.
4. Add **pepper and chili flakes**.
5. Mix well and stir fry for **2–3 minutes**.

---

### 6. Final Touch
- Add minced **spring onions** on top.
- Taste and adjust seasoning if needed.

---

## Meal Prep Portioning

Divide the fried rice into **4 equal containers**.

Each meal roughly contains:
- ~35–40 g protein
- balanced carbs
- high fiber vegetables

---

## Storage

- **Refrigerator:** up to 4 days
- **Freezer:** up to 2 weeks

Reheat in microwave or pan before eating.

---

## Tips for Healthier Fried Rice

- Use **brown rice** for more fiber.
- Add **broccoli or mushrooms** for more nutrients.
- Use **minimal oil** for lower calories.
- Day-old rice works best for texture.
