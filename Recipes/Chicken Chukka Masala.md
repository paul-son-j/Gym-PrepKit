# Home Made Chicken Chukka Masala

Best dish to prepare at Friday Night.

## Ingredients
### Protein
- 750 g Chicken
- 100 ml curd

### Vegetables
- 3 onions medium size (minced)
- 2 tomato medium size (pasted)
- 2 table spoon Ginger Garlic paste

### Masalas
- 2 table spoon Coriander powder
- 2 table spoon Chilly powder
- 1 table spoon Cumin powder
- 1 table spoon Garam masala
- 1 table spoon Turmeric powder

### Cooking Oil/Butter
- 3 table spoon

## Preparation

### Marination
1. Clean the chicken.
2. Add turmeric powder 1 table spoon + curd 100ml + Ginger Garlic paste 1 table spoon.
3. Marinate it for 30 minutes.

### Cooking
1. Add oil/butter to pan.
2. Add ginger garlic paste remaining.
3. Add onions.
4. Saute the onions till golden brown.
5. Add tomato paste.
6. Saute it till the paste gets dried.
7. Add the chilly powder, cumin powder, coriander powder.
8. Add the marinated chicken in medium flame for 5 min.
9. Slow cook it for 15–20 min.
10. Add the garam masala and required salt.
11. Cook it for 2 min.

## Meal Prep Portioning

Divide the masala into **4 equal containers**.

Each meal roughly contains:
- ~35–40 g protein
- rich in spices and antioxidants

---

## Storage

- **Refrigerator:** up to 3 days
- **Freezer:** up to 2 weeks

Reheat in microwave or pan before eating.

---

## Tips

- Use **bone-in chicken** for more flavour.
- For tomato paste, add cashew + coriander for flavours.
- Add the coriander at last for smell.
- Marinate overnight for deeper taste.
- Adjust chilly powder to your spice preference.
- Pair with rice, roti, or parotta.
